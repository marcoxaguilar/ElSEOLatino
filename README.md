# ElSEOLatino.com

Marketing digital con inteligencia artificial para negocios latinos en Estados Unidos.

## 🚀 About

ElSEOLatino is a bilingual digital marketing agency specializing in AI-powered SEO, cinematic drone videography, web design, and branded merchandise for Latino-owned businesses across the U.S.

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **HTML5 / CSS3 / Vanilla JS** — No frameworks, no dependencies, blazing fast
- **Google Fonts** — Playfair Display, DM Sans, Space Mono
- **CSS Animations** — Scroll-reveal, comparison bar fills, scanning line effects
- **Responsive** — Mobile-first, works on all screen sizes

## 🖥 Sections

1. **Navbar** — Fixed with smooth scroll, gold CTA, mobile hamburger menu
2. **Hero** — AI-powered headline, stats, floating service cards
3. **AI Advantage** — Dedicated section: predictive analysis, AI content, 24/7 monitoring + animated comparison bars
4. **Servicios** — SEO con IA, Web Design, Ropa/Merch, Consultoría, **Drone Videos** (full-width featured card with camera viewfinder UI)
5. **Nosotros** — Why choose us, bilingual, AI + drone differentiators
6. **Testimonios** — 3 Latino business owner reviews (Houston, Chicago, Miami)
7. **CTA** — Free consultation with phone, WhatsApp, and email buttons
8. **Footer** — Contact info, nav links, branding
